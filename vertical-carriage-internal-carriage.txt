Vertical carriage - internal carriage notes
===========================================

Slice: Course & Solid
Feedrate: 80
Flowrate: 100

Drill out the bearing rod holes to slightly less than 8 mm (7.5 mm for example) so that the rods are a tight fit.

Drill out the swivel screw holes to 2.5 mm and tap using an M3 tap.

Drill out the rod end bracket holes to 3 mm.
